## Contact Us

We welcome your questions, feedback, and inquiries. Please use the appropriate channel below to ensure the fastest response.

---

### Editorial & Content

For corrections, factual errors, content suggestions, or feedback on any review:

**Email**: [editorial@stackinsider.org](mailto:editorial@stackinsider.org)

If you work for a vendor and believe a review contains an error, please include:
- The specific claim or statement in question
- The correct information with a verifiable source
- Your name and role at the vendor

We review factual correction requests within **5 business days** and update content if the correction is warranted.

---

### Advertising & Partnerships

For advertising inquiries, sponsored content opportunities, or partnership proposals:

**Email**: [advertising@stackinsider.org](mailto:advertising@stackinsider.org)

Please include "Advertising" in your email subject line. Note that we do not accept payment to write favorable reviews or to modify review conclusions. Sponsored content, if accepted, will be clearly labeled.

---

### Privacy & Data Requests

To exercise your GDPR, CCPA, or other data protection rights, or for privacy-related questions:

**Email**: [privacy@stackinsider.org](mailto:privacy@stackinsider.org)

Please see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) for detailed information about how we handle your data.

---

### General Inquiries

For anything else — media requests, speaking invitations, collaborations, or general questions:

**Email**: [contact@stackinsider.org](mailto:contact@stackinsider.org)

---

### Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within **48 business hours**. Privacy and data rights requests are handled according to the timelines specified in our Privacy Policy.
