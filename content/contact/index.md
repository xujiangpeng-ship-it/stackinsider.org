## Get in Touch with the StackInsider Team

We appreciate your interest in StackInsider. Whether you have a question about our reviews, want to report a factual error, or are exploring partnership opportunities, we aim to respond within 48 business hours.

---

### Editorial & Content Corrections

We take accuracy seriously. If you spot an error in any review — outdated pricing, incorrect feature claims, or broken links — please let us know:

**Email**: [editorial@stackinsider.org](mailto:editorial@stackinsider.org)

When reporting a factual error, please include:
- The specific claim or statement in question
- The correct information with a verifiable source (vendor documentation, official press release, etc.)
- The URL of the affected article

We review factual correction requests within **5 business days** and update content promptly if the correction is warranted. All corrections are logged and visible to readers.

---

### Advertising & Partnership Inquiries

For advertising opportunities, sponsored content proposals, or affiliate partnerships:

**Email**: [advertising@stackinsider.org](mailto:advertising@stackinsider.org)

Please include "Advertising Inquiry" in your email subject line and describe:
- Your company name and the product/service you promote
- The type of partnership you are proposing (display ads, sponsored content, affiliate program, etc.)
- Your target audience and expected traffic levels

**Important**: StackInsider maintains strict editorial independence. We do not accept payment to write favorable reviews or to modify review conclusions. Any sponsored content will be clearly labeled as such and will undergo the same editorial review process as our organic content.

### Vendor Relations

If you represent a software vendor and would like to:
- Provide corrections to pricing or feature information on our pages
- Share product updates, release notes, or documentation
- Request removal of inaccurate claims based on verified sources
- Discuss product demos or trial accounts for our editorial team

**Email**: [vendor@stackinsider.org](mailto:vendor@stackinsider.org)

We welcome accurate, timely information from vendors. Our reviews are based on publicly available data and hands-on testing, and we always verify claims against official documentation.

### Privacy & Data Protection Requests

To exercise your rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other data protection regulations:

**Email**: [privacy@stackinsider.org](mailto:privacy@stackinsider.org)

Please specify which right you are exercising (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection) and include:
- Your full name
- The email address associated with your account or inquiry
- A description of the data or processing activity in question

We respond to data protection requests within **30 days** as required by law. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) for complete details.

### Frequently Asked Questions About Contacting Us

**How long does it take to get a response?**  
We respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. Editorial corrections are prioritized and typically addressed within 5 business days.

**Can I request a review of a specific software?**  
Yes. Email [editorial@stackinsider.org](mailto:editorial@stackinsider.org) with the software name and your use case. We consider reader requests when planning our editorial calendar.

**Do you accept guest posts?**  
Currently, StackInsider does not accept unsolicited guest posts. All content is produced by our editorial team to maintain consistency and editorial standards.

**How do I report a broken link or technical issue on the site?**  
Email [tech@stackinsider.org](mailto:tech@stackinsider.org) with the affected URL and a description of the issue. We fix technical problems within 24 hours of notification.
