## Our Commitment

StackInsider publishes independent, unbiased reviews of B2B software. We do not accept payment for reviews, and our editorial team makes all coverage decisions independently.

## Review Process

Every review on StackInsider follows a three-stage editorial process:

1. **Research & Testing**: Each product is hands-on tested by a practitioner with domain expertise. We evaluate core features, pricing transparency, user experience, and real-world implementation viability.

2. **Peer Review**: Drafts undergo internal peer review by at least one other team member to verify factual accuracy and completeness.

3. **Editorial Review**: A senior editor reviews the final draft for clarity, structure, and adherence to our editorial standards.

## Sources & Methodology

We rely on a combination of primary sources (hands-on testing, vendor documentation, API evaluation) and secondary sources (user reviews on platforms like G2, Capterra, and Gartner Peer Insights; industry reports; and practitioner forums). All external sources are cited inline.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on StackInsider may be affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial rankings or review conclusions.

## Corrections

If you find an error in any of our reviews, please contact us at **corrections@stackinsider.org**. We commit to reviewing and, if verified, correcting errors within 72 hours.

## Contact

For editorial inquiries, reach us at **editorial@stackinsider.org**.
