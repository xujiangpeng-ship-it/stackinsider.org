Gufei.Sun is the founder and lead analyst at StackInsider. With over a decade of hands-on experience implementing and evaluating enterprise software at Fortune 500 companies and mid-market firms alike, he brings a practitioner's lens to every review.

## Background

Before founding StackInsider, Gufei spent 8 years as an enterprise solutions architect, leading ERP and CRM deployments across manufacturing, retail, and professional services verticals. He has managed software selection processes with budgets ranging from $50K to $5M, giving him firsthand insight into the gap between vendor promises and real-world implementation.

## Expertise

- **ERP Systems**: Deep knowledge of SAP, Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, and Acumatica
- **CRM Software**: Extensive experience with Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, and Pipedrive
- **TCO Analysis**: Rigorous methodology for calculating true total cost of ownership, including hidden implementation, training, and integration costs
- **Vendor Negotiation**: Insider perspective on pricing models, discount structures, and contract pitfalls

## Methodology

Every review on StackInsider follows a structured evaluation framework that Gufei developed over years of real-world software procurement. The framework scores products across six dimensions: functional depth, usability, pricing transparency, integration capability, vendor support quality, and scalability.
