## About

Gufei Sun is a B2B software consultant and former enterprise solutions architect with over 10 years of experience evaluating, implementing, and migrating ERP, accounting, and project management platforms for mid-market and enterprise companies.

He has personally set up or migrated **four different accounting systems** across construction, consulting, and SaaS businesses — giving him first-hand insight into what actually works on job sites versus what looks good in demos.

## Expertise

- **Construction Accounting**: Job costing, prevailing wage, AIA billing, retention management
- **ERP Migration**: QuickBooks → Sage/NetSuite/Microsoft Dynamics 360
- **AP Automation**: Invoice processing workflows, approval routing, 2/3-way matching
- **Budgeting & Forecasting**: Rolling forecasts, driver-based planning, scenario modeling
- **Software Evaluation**: RFP management, vendor negotiations, TCO analysis
