## About

Sarah Okafor is a CPA and financial technology specialist who has spent her career at the intersection of accounting operations and software implementation. She specializes in helping finance teams evaluate tools that reduce close time, improve audit trails, and eliminate spreadsheet dependency.

## Expertise

- **Financial Close Management**: Month-end, quarter-end, and year-end processes
- **Compliance & Audit**: SOX readiness, audit trail requirements, segregation of duties
- **Process Automation**: Eliminating manual data entry and reconciliation workarounds
- **Vendor Evaluation**: Total cost of ownership, hidden fee analysis, contract negotiation

## Philosophy

> "The best software isn't the one with the most features — it's the one your team will actually use every day."
