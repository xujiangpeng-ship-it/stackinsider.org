## About

David Park is a former IT director turned software analyst who specializes in integration architecture, data migration, and vendor-neutral evaluations. He brings the infrastructure perspective to every review — because even the best accounting software is useless if it can't talk to your CRM.

## Expertise

- **System Integration**: API connectivity, middleware, iPaaS evaluation
- **Data Architecture**: Master data management, data quality, ETL pipelines
- **Security & Compliance**: SOC 2, GDPR, role-based access control
- **Vendor Assessment**: Technical due diligence, scalability analysis, exit strategy planning

## Philosophy

> "Integration isn't an afterthought — it's the reason most software projects fail or succeed."
