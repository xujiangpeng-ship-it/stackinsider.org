## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 30, 2026

At **Stack Insider** (https://stackinsider.org), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

### Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. However, third-party services used on this website may collect certain data automatically.

### Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

This website displays advertisements served by **Google AdSense**, a third-party advertising network. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites.

- **Cookies** are small text files stored on your device that help Google deliver more relevant ads.
- **Web beacons** are invisible graphics that help Google understand user behavior for ad serving purposes.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
