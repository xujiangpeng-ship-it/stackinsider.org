## Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

### What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites function properly, provide analytics, and personalize your experience.

### Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (required for site functionality):
- Theme preference (light/dark mode) — stored in localStorage
- Session cookies for contact form submissions

**Analytics Cookies**:
- Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat) — tracks visitor counts, page views, and bounce rates in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is collected.
- We do not use analytics cookies for behavioral advertising or user profiling.

**Advertising Cookies**:
- Google AdSense cookies (test_ads, NID, IDE) — serve relevant ads based on your browsing patterns across sites. You can opt out at [adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com).
- These cookies do not collect personal data from this Site.

**Third-Party Cookies**:
- Google Fonts — loads typography from Google CDN
- YouTube embeds — if any videos are embedded, they may set cookies per YouTube policy

### Your Choices

- **Browser settings**: You can configure your browser to reject all cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. Note that rejecting essential cookies may impair site functionality.
- **Google Ads opt-out**: Visit [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) to opt out of personalized advertising across Google network.
- **Do Not Track**: We respect DNT signals and will not serve behavioral ads from browsers that send them.

### Cookie Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
