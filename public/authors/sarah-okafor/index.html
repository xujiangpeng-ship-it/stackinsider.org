Sarah Okafor is an industry analyst at StackInsider, where she leads market intelligence and competitive landscape research. Her work ensures that every review is contextualized within broader market dynamics — not just feature comparisons, but an understanding of where each product fits in the ecosystem.

Background

Sarah spent 6 years as a market research analyst at a leading B2B technology research firm, where she authored over 200 market reports covering SaaS, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise applications. She has tracked vendor market share shifts, pricing model evolutions, and M&A activity across the CRM, ERP, and project management sectors.

Expertise

  • SaaS Market Tracking: Monitoring vendor funding rounds, product launches, and strategic pivots
  • Pricing Trend Analysis: Tracking how SaaS pricing models evolve — per-user to usage-based, freemium expansion, and platform plays
  • Competitive Intelligence: Mapping feature parity matrices and identifying differentiated capabilities
  • Buyer Persona Research: Understanding how different organizational profiles evaluate and adopt software

Contributions

Sarah’s market context sections in StackInsider reviews help readers understand not just whether a tool is good, but whether it’s gaining or losing ground, how its pricing stacks up against the market average, and what its roadmap signals about long-term viability.
